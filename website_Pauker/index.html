Welcome to Stray Kids!

Stray Kids (Korean: 스트레이 키즈, RR: Seuteurei Kijeu; often abbreviated to SKZ) is a South Korean boy band formed in 2017 with 9 members. Kim Woojin left in late 2019 due to undisclosed personal reasons. The current members (from oldest to youngest) are: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Seo Changbin, Hwang Hyunjin, Han Jisung, Lee Felix, Kim Seungmin and Yang Jeongin. All their songs were self-produced by the members. They are under contract with JYP Entertainment.
