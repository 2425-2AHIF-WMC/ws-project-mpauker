Members

Bang Chan

Chan is the leader and was able to choose the members of Stray Kids himself. He is part of "3Racha", a group of three producers that had already formed before Stray Kids. The name comes from a wordplay on the hot sauce sriracha.

Birthday: October 3, 1997

Skzoo (animal mascot): Wolf Chan (wolf)

Lee Know

Lee Know (real name: Lee Minho) is the main dancer of the group and has three cats, Soonie, Doongie and Dori.

Birthday: October 25, 1998
